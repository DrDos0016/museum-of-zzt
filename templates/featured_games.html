Featured games, are games which have been deemed unique in some way as an example of what ZZT has to offer. These game worlds generally show a higher quality than most, and have been given their place in the spotlight.

In the past, a Game of the Month award was given to worlds which were deemed the best released that month. A Classic Game of the Month award was given to a game released at any time. Eventually these awards ceased, until years later when Featured Games brought the idea of the Classic Game of the Month back.

Additionally, a renowned community member MadTom ran his own award series, MadTom's Pick, which filled the role of the Game of the Month.

All of these worlds that received these awards along with their reviews have been merged into the Featured Games list. If you would like to write about a game which showcases excellence in ZZT in some way, please do! This list is far from complete and could certainly use new additions.
