To run: - Download ZZT v3.2 from https://museumofzzt.com/file/view/zzt/ - Extract ZZT and any worlds you wish you play into a directory - Download the latest version of Zeta from https://zeta.asie.pl - Extract Zeta into the directory containing ZZT and your ZZT worlds - Run zeta86.exe and configure ZZT (You'll likely want "K"eyboard and "C"olor) - Press W to bring up the world list and ENTER to select a world - Press P to play!
