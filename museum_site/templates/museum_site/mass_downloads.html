By today's standards, ZZT's worlds are incredible small downloads. A complete collection of all files hosted on the Museum of ZZT isn't that much larger than 100 MB.

Because of their small size, automatically updated bulk downloads are made available to download. Due to potential collisions with file names (as well as issues with ZZT trying to load a list of hundreds of worlds), they have been split up into categories, for non-ZZT worlds, and by release year for ZZT worlds.

Note that since these downloads consist of nothing more than a zip file filled with dozens of zip files you may wind up downloading plenty of duplicate copies of executables, fonts, or other content.
