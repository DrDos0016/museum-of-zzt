z2 - The ZZT Archive

Welcome to z2. A website originally created for the ZZT community to share their games and interact with fellow ZZTers. After years of stagnation, the site has been redeveloped from scratch with a new purpose of historical preservation of everything ZZT.

If you've never played ZZT before, or haven't touched it in years, check out the Getting Started With ZZT article, to get yourself ready to experience 25 years of a gaming subculture unlike any other.
