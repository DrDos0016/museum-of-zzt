Publication Packs are articles which give a brief overview of uploads to the Museum of ZZT which have been properly published to its archives.

These articles mostly exist as a record of what was published and when. Most uploads are only given a cursory glance and summary based on initial impressions with a selection of hand-picked screenshots of the content contained within. Information is meant to be small enough to be reproducible on the Worlds of ZZT Twitter in thread form.
